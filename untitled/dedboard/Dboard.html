What is a CTF

Capture the Flag (CTF) is a competition that related to information security where the participants will be test on a various of security challenges like web penetration testing, reverse engineering, cryptography, steganography and few others more. Participants must get the “flag” to gain their points. So, the team who have the highest point at the end of the game will be the winner;
